#predicate 

>[!iparam]
>1. $A \in \textsf{Set}$

>[!param]
>2. $p \in \mathbb{B} \sim A$

>[!assume]
>3. [[Predicate]] $p$

>[!define]
>4. $\mathop{{\textsf{one}}} p \; \equiv \; (\exists x \mid \mathop{p} x : (\forall y \mid \mathop{p} y : y = x))$ #needs-writeup 

>[!observe]
>5. $\mathop{{\textsf{one}}} p \; \equiv \; (\exists x \mid : (\forall y \mid \mathop{p} y \equiv y = x))$ #needs-writeup 
>6. $\mathop{{\textsf{one}}} p \; \equiv \; (\exists x \mid: \mathop{p} x) \wedge (\forall y, z \mid \mathop{p} y \wedge \mathop{p} z : y = z))$ #needs-writeup 

>[!observe]
>7. $\mathop{{\textsf{one}}} p \equiv$ [[Lone|lone]] $p \; \wedge$  [[Some|some]] $p$ #needs-writeup
