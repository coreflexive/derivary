>[!iparam]
>1. $A \in \textsf{Set}$

>[!param]
>1. $R \in A \sim A$
>2. $X \in \textsf{Set}$

>[!assume]
>3. $X \subseteq A$
>4. $(\forall a, b \mid a \mathbin{R} b \wedge b \in X : a \in X)$

>[!observe]
>1. $X = R[X] \Rightarrow$ [[Left closed]] $R$ $X$
>2. ( [[Left closed]] $R$ $X$ $\Rightarrow X = R[X]$ ) $\Leftarrow$ [[Reflexive]] $R$
>3. ( [[Left closed]] $R$ $X$ $\equiv X = R[X]$ ) $\Leftarrow$ [[Reflexive]] $R$
